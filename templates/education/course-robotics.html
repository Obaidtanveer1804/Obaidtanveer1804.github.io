Robotics Fundamentals

YouTube

Course Details: YouTube

Duration: 2 months (2021)

Overview: This course introduced Robotics, blending theory with practical applications, focusing on embedded systems integration with mechanical components.

Key Learnings:

  • Studied kinematics for Robotics arms and mobile robots.
  • Implemented PID control for motor precision.
  • Integrated Arduino with ultrasonic and IR sensors.
  • Explored A* and Dijkstra path planning.
  • Learned OpenCV for computer vision.
  • Prototyped with 3D-printed parts.

Projects: Built a 4-DOF Robotics arm with Arduino, including 3D-printed parts, inverse kinematics, and a PC-based GUI.

Challenges: Addressed kinematic singularities and mitigated servo jitter with PID tuning.
