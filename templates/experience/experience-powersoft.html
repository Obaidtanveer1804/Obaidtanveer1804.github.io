Embedded Firmware Engineer

Powersoft

Duration: Nov 2024 - Present

Responsibilities: At Powersoft, I’m contributing to cutting-edge embedded firmware projects for electric vehicle (EV) systems, battery management, and charging infrastructure. My key contributions include:

  • Developed firmware for advanced STM32 microcontrollers, including dual-core STM32H747ZI (M7/M4 cores), STM32H755ZI-Q, STM32H743ZI, and F0 Discovery boards, optimizing for performance and power efficiency.
  • Implemented FreeV2G for EV charging on Raspberry Pi 4 with Raspbian, using Python with Whitebeet boards and OpenEVSE, enabling vehicle-to-grid communication protocols.
  • Designed and developed an L2 EV charger from scratch using STM32H747, integrating CANopen and ISO-SPI for robust communication with EV systems.
  • Built battery management systems (BMS) for Li-ion cells, handling cell balancing, state-of-charge (SoC) estimation, and thermal management using CAN, ISO-SPI, UART, SPI, and ADCs.
  • Utilized CAN sniffers and Saleae logic analyzers for in-depth debugging of communication protocols, ensuring compliance with automotive standards.
  • Developed a deep understanding of inverters, implementing control algorithms for efficient power conversion in EV charging systems.
  • Designed and implemented a simple bootloader for STM32, enabling over-the-air (OTA) firmware updates, and gained expertise in STM32 memory structure (Flash, RAM, and peripherals).
  • Worked on BLE integration for remote monitoring of EV chargers, using STM32’s built-in peripherals for low-power communication.
  • Collaborated with hardware teams to design and validate PCBs for BMS and charging systems, ensuring signal integrity and EMI compliance.
  • Performed system-level testing under real-world conditions, simulating high-load scenarios to validate BMS and charger reliability, achieving zero failures over 1000 test cycles.

This role has significantly expanded my expertise in EV systems, BMS, and advanced microcontroller applications. I’ve gained in-depth knowledge of automotive communication protocols, power electronics, and system-level design, aligning with my passion for sustainable technologies and preparing me for leadership roles in embedded engineering.
